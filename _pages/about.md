Roberto M. Fuentes received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Engineering Science at the University of Talca, Curicó, Chile, in 2021 and 2023, respectively. He is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Engineering Systems at the University of Talca, Curicó, Chile.
His research interests mainly include robust stability analysis and control of uncertain sampled-data linear systems, and markov jump linear systems.
